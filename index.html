How Does MenoPhix Work?

MenoPhix is designed to naturally boost estrogen levels in the brain, which is essential as women age and experience menopause, a time when estrogen levels, particularly in the brain, can significantly decrease, leading to various symptoms.

Formulated with carefully chosen herbs and plants, MenoPhix enhances the activity of aromatase enzymes in the body, which are responsible for converting testosterone into estrogen. By boosting aromatase activity, MenoPhix helps raise estrogen levels, especially in the brain, alleviating menopausal symptoms.

Key ingredients like muira puama, sarsaparilla, and ashwagandha are rich in sterols that support aromatase enzyme activity and estrogen production.

Through this natural restoration of estrogen balance, MenoPhix targets symptoms such as hot flashes, night sweats, mood swings, brain fog, low libido, and other common menopausal issues.
